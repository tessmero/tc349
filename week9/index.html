HeatMap Editor

The Heatmap editor is a suit of visualization tools that centers around the visualization of value spreadsheets in heatmaps.

Researchers interested in developing publishable figures will understand the amount of time that goes into prototyping. Developing and tweaking heatmaps in R, for example, often requires running a similar command hunderds of times until the desired result is achieved. Users of the Heatmap Editor are provided with the ability to make such adjustments in real-time.
